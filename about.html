About Uttarakhand Technical University, Dehradun

The Uttarakhand Technical University, Dehradun was established on 27th January 2005 by Govt. of Uttarakhand through the Uttarakhand Technical University Act 2005. The Uttarakhand Technical University campus is situated at NH-72 Suddhowala, Dehradun. Dehradun is the State capital of Uttarakhand well connected through Rail, Road and Air transport.

The university offered various courses specially B.Tech/M.Tech in different branches. The B.Tech/M.Tech course offered different types of theory as well as practical examination. As per University rules the practical examination held at college campus by external examiner.
